[h1][b]Giant Doors[/b][/h1]
[b]Built for kings, giants and 4 high rooms...[/b]

[h1]Description[/h1]
Ever wanted a larger door to separate rooms, or just for general looks?

This mod brings the large door reality to life with a rotatable 4 block tall pneumatic door & mechanized airlock.

[h2]Mod Version: [b]1.0.3[/b][/h2]
[h2]Latest Tested Game Version:[b] CS-444111[/b][/h2]

[h1]Specifics[/h1]
[list]
    [*] Adds 2 new doors to the base menu in pneumatic and mechanized variants.
    [*] Doors can be built out of the same materials as their counterparts (400 cost).
    [*] Unlocked through the employment & refined objects research nodes.
    [*] Fully customizable speed through Config file.
[/list]

[h1]Options[/h1]
Configuration of this mod and a basic template can be found in the following locations:
[list]
[*][b]Windows[/b] •  ~\Documents\Klei\OxygenNotIncluded\mods\Steam\1882896929\Config.json
[*][b]Mac[/b] •  ~/Library/Application Support/unity.Klei.Oxygen Not Included/mods/Steam/1882896929\Config.json
[/list]

[b]Door Speeds[/b] - Animation speed modifiers for each door variant. Used to speed up/slow down door opening/closing to your liking. Must be a float between 0 and 15. (Default: 1.0, 0.9, 1.2)
[list]
[*][i][b]Disclaimer:[/b] In the case where invalid arguments are given the default values will be used.[/i]
[/list]
