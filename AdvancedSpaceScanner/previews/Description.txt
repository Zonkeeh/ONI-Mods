[h1][b]Advanced Space Scanner[/b][/h1]
[b]QoL new late game alternative to the space scanner! [/b]

[h1]Description[/h1]
Have you ever wanted to have a set and forget space scanner, that costs a little extra but require less thought/up keep?

This mod provides a new bunker style space scanner that automatically protects itself when something approaches.

[h2]Mod Version: [b]1.0.5[/b][/h2]
[h2]Latest Tested Game Version: [b]CS-444111[/b][/h2]

[h1]Specifics[/h1]
[list]
    [*] Gives a meteor proof (5 high and 6 wide) space scanner with custom anims.
    [*] Appears next to the space scanner in the building menu.
    [*] Fully customisable material cost and scanning configuration.
[/list]

[h1]Configuration[/h1]
[i]Config.json file can be found in:
[list]
[*] Windows: ~\Documents\Klei\OxygenNotIncluded\mods\Steam\1873884303\
[*] Mac:  ~/Library/Application Support/unity.Klei.Oxygen Not Included/mods/Steam/1873884303\
[/list]

A template file Config.json.template is provided, simply copy, remove the .template from the filename and remove the comments (//).[/i]

Config has the following variables:
[list]
    [*] RefinedMetalCost - [i]Configurable amount for how much refined metal to use[/i] (default: 150)
    [*] GlassCost - [i]Configurable amount for how much glass to use[/i] (default: 25)
    [*] DoesRegolithSpawn - [i]Option will change if regolith can spawn on the space scanner.[/i] (default: false)
    [*] IsOverheatable - [i]Option will change if the space scanner can be overheated.[/i] (default: false)
    [*] MinimumWarningTime - [i]Minimum warning time in seconds that the scanner can have.[/i] (default:40)
    [*] MaximumWarningTime - [i]Maximum warning time in seconds that the scanner can have.[/i] (default:300)
    [*] InterferenceRadius - [i]Interference radius of the scanner (how far away machines must be).[/i] (default:4)
[/list]
